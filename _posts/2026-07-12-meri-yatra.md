Guwahati Zoo Ki Yaadgaar Yatra – 25 June 2026

25 June 2026 ko mujhe Guwahati Zoo ghumne ka mauka mila. Yeh din mere liye bahut hi yaadgaar raha. Guwahati Zoo, jise Assam State Zoo bhi kaha jata hai, Assam ke sabse sundar aur prasiddh paryatan sthalon mein se ek hai. Har saal yahan desh ke alag-alag hisson se hazaron tourist ghumne aate hain.

Zoo ka vatavaran bahut hi shant aur prakriti se bhara hua hai. Hariyali, bade-bade ped aur khula mahol ise aur bhi khubsurat bana dete hain. Yahan pahunchte hi man ko ek alag hi sukoon milta hai.

Zoo ke andar bahut saare janwar aur pakshi dekhne ko milte hain. Maine yahan bear (bhalu), zebra, hiran, alag-alag prajati ke pakshi aur kai anya jeev dekhe. Har janwar ko dekhkar bachche aur bade sabhi bahut khush nazar aa rahe the. Janwaron ko unke prakritik jaise vatavaran mein dekhna ek bahut hi anokha anubhav tha.

Zoo ke andar motor se chalne wali gaadi ki bhi suvidha hai. Bahut se log is gaadi mein baithkar poore zoo ka anand lete hain aur aasani se alag-alag janwaron ko dekh pate hain. Yeh suvidha khaaskar bachchon aur buzurgon ke liye bahut upyogi hai.

Yahan ka entry fee Bharatiya paryatak ke liye kaafi sasta hai, isliye har koi aasani se yahan ghumne aa sakta hai. Zoo itna bada hai ki 3 se 4 ghante paidal ghumne ke baad bhi lagta hai ki abhi bahut kuch dekhna baki hai.

Maine dekha ki bahut se log apne friends aur family ke sath yahan aaye hue the. Sab log milkar tasveerein le rahe the, janwaron ko dekh rahe the aur apna samay khushi se bita rahe the. Sach kahun to group mein ghumne ka maza aur bhi badh jata hai.

Agar aap kabhi Guwahati aayen, to ek baar Guwahati Zoo zarur ghumne jaiye. Apne parivar ya doston ke sath yahan aane par aapko bahut maza aayega aur aap prakriti aur vanya jeevon ke kareeb hone ka ek khoobsurat anubhav lekar lautenge.

Yeh yatra mere liye bahut hi yaadgaar rahi aur main dobara yahan aana chahunga.
