The person behind the postcards

Hi, main Lebnush hoon.

Ek bag, ek notebook, aur ek badi si list of un jagahon ki jahan abhi jaana baaki hai.

journeyByLebnush shuru hua ek chhoti si aadat se — har trip ke baad apni notebook mein woh cheezein likhna jo photo mein nahi aatin. Woh chai wale ka naam, station pe suni ek adhoori kahani, ya bas woh feeling jab kisi naye sheher mein pehli baar kadam rakhte hain.

Pichle kuch saalon mein maine Rajasthan ki galiyon se lekar Bali ke rice terraces tak, aur Istanbul ke do mahaadveepon ke beech safar kiya hai. Har jagah ne kuch sikhaya — kabhi dheeraj, kabhi hausla, aur kabhi bas yeh ki kho jaana bhi theek hai.

Yeh blog ek safarnama hai — kisi guidebook se zyada, ek diary jaisa. Agar aap bhi safar karna pasand karte hain, ya bas ek cup chai ke saath kisi aur ki kahaniyan padhna chahte hain, toh aap sahi jagah par hain.
